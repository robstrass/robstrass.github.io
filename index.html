About Me

I'm a curious and hardworking professional that loves learning and finding solutions to complex problems. My path into Software Engineering is anything but ordinary. I received a BS in Criminal Justice with the intent of getting into law enforcement, but decided to pivot shortly after college, trying out a few ventures to find what suited me best.

I tried my hand in Sales because I'm naturally not very extroverted, and wanted to prove to myself that I could do it. While hitting an average of 120% to quota in my sales role, I also started my own business where I provided marketing solutions to small business owners. The COVID-19 pandemic forced me to table that venture, and sales felt emotionally and professionally unfulfilling.

With my freetime in the pandemic, I decided to learn how to code, and my love of the problem solving aspect of programming captivated me. I enrolled in App Academy shortly after, quit my job, and went all to become a Software Engineer. I now have 2 years of fullstack development experience working at both a networking startup and a multinational insurance company.

Outside of work, one of my passions is landscape photography. In fact, the pictures here were taken by me while traveling Europe with my sister after college. I love landscape photography because being out in nature and seeing how beautiful the world is helps me ground myself and appreciate life.



Skills

I'm proficient in programming in TypeScript, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and also have a familiarity with Python and SQL. While in my last role, I worked with React, Redux thunks and sagas, Express, Node.js, and many smaller libraries, including Bluebird and Axios for asynchronous functionality, and Lodash for commonly used programming tasks.

I also actively participated in daily team standups to discuss blockers and current tasks, along with regular code reviews. I also worked in pair programming settings in my last role and while attending App Academy.
